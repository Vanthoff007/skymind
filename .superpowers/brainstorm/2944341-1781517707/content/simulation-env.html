What simulation environment are you targeting?

This shapes the entire stack — ROS integration, physics fidelity, and how easily you can deploy to real hardware later.

A

Gazebo + ROS 2

You already use ROS 2 in skynet-ws. Strong community support, good sensor simulation (depth, RGB, IMU), works with PX4/ArduPilot SITL. Most transferable to real hardware.

B

AirSim / Microsoft AirSim (Unreal)

Photorealistic indoor environments, excellent for vision-based tasks. Harder ROS bridge, higher GPU cost. Best for training/testing vision models.

C

Habitat-Sim (Meta)

Purpose-built for embodied AI navigation research. Ships with semantically rich indoor scenes (HM3D, Gibson). Native integration with foundation model benchmarks. Less realistic physics.
